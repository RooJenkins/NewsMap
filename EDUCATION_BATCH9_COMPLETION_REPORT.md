### Fragile/Conflict States
- **Somalia, Sudan, Libya:** Education systems collapsed or severely disrupted by conflict
- Millions of children out of school
- Infrastructure destruction and teacher displacement

### Learning Crisis Countries
- **Mozambique:** 92% of Grade 3 students cannot read
- **South Africa:** Last place in TIMSS despite highest spending in Africa
- **Morocco:** 73-76% below PISA baseline proficiency

### Access vs. Quality Gaps
- **Algeria, Ghana:** Near-universal enrollment but poor learning outcomes
- Rote memorization over critical thinking
- Teacher quality remains core challenge

### Resource vs. Results Paradoxes
- **South Africa:** 6.4% of GDP spent, worst learning outcomes
- **DRC:** 1% of GDP spent, parent-financed system
- **Angola:** Oil wealth not translating to education investment

### Language Policy Challenges
- **Morocco, Algeria, Senegal:** Arabic/French/indigenous language tensions
- Students learning in languages they don't speak at home
- Cognitive burden undermining subject learning
