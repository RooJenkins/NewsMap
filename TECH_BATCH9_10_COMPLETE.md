- Brain drain to higher-paying markets (€35-45k local vs €70-90k Berlin)
- Barcelona Mobile World Congress hub

### **Sri Lanka**
- Clawing back from 2022 economic crisis
- $1.5B IT/BPO exports, 120k workers
- Catastrophic brain drain: 25k tech workers fled 2022-2024
- Remote work as lifeline for remaining talent
- Ransomware attack on national ID database

### **Sudan**
- Tech sector virtually destroyed by civil war
- Internet penetration collapsed from 31% to 12%
- Total brain drain of tech talent
- Telecommunications infrastructure decimated
- No path forward without peace

### **Sweden**
- One of Europe's most successful tech ecosystems
- More unicorns per capita than anywhere except US/Israel
- Stockholm: €4.5B VC funding in 2024
- Acute talent shortage despite immigration
- Cybersecurity national priority after Russian attacks

### **Switzerland**
- Elite but niche: deep tech, fintech, crypto
- ETH Zurich/EPFL driving innovation
- Crypto Valley struggling in crypto winter
- Astronomical cost of living limiting talent
- World-class cybersecurity infrastructure

### **Syria**
- Tech sector obliterated by 14 years of civil war
- 50,000+ tech workers fled abroad
- Internet heavily censored, electricity 2-4 hours daily
- Assad regime uses tech for surveillance/repression
- Digital infrastructure in ruins

### **Taiwan**
- World's most geopolitically critical tech sector
- 92% of advanced semiconductors (sub-7nm) made here
- TSMC: $650B market cap, indispensable to global supply chains
- Constant Chinese cyberattacks
- Cannot replicate ecosystem quickly despite US/Japan expansion

### **Tanzania**
- Rising East African tech hub
- Mobile money: 25M users, $50B annual transactions
- $85M VC funding in 2024
- Heavy-handed online content regulations ($930/year blogger fees)
- Electricity access only 43% nationally

### **Thailand**
- Southeast Asia's underperformer
- Only $1.2B VC funding vs Indonesia's $3.5B
- PromptPay: 57M users, billions in transactions
- Political instability and coups hampering growth
- No comprehensive data protection law

### **Tunisia**
- Tech sector collapsing under authoritarianism
- 10,000+ tech workers emigrated since 2021
- VC funding collapsed to under $30M
- President Saied's power grab devastating ecosystem
- Brain drain gutting engineering talent

### **Turkey**
- Enormous potential strangled by authoritarianism
- 30,000+ tech workers emigrated 2020-2024
- Hyperinflation and lira collapse (32:1 to USD)
- Social media censorship, arrests for criticism
- Massive cryptocurrency adoption amid crisis

### **Uganda**
- Small but scrappy ecosystem
- Mobile money: 20M users, $30B annual transactions
- Social media tax and internet shutdowns during elections
- Computer Misuse Act criminalizing online speech
- Electricity access only 45%

### **Ukraine**
- Miracle of resilience under fire
- $7.3B IT services exports despite war
- 100,000+ tech workers emigrated since 2022
- Remarkable wartime innovation (drones, cyber defense)
- Future depends on defeating Russia
